What is a Personal Chef?

A personal chef is a trained chef who comes into clients’ homes and creates personalized meals for them and their families for a fee. The Chef will do the meal planning, shopping, cooking, and cleaning and all the client has to do is enjoy the meals! Personal chefs take all the guesswork out of the dreaded “What’s for dinner?” question.

Typical clients include families with a packed calendar, professionals who barely have time to pour a cup of coffee, amateur chefs who want to broaden their foodie knowledge, or even people who want to eat better but don’t want to have to think about it.
